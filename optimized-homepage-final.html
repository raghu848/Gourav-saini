Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our orthopedic treatments and procedures

What is robotic knee replacement surgery?

Robotic knee replacement is an advanced surgical technique that uses computer-assisted technology to precisely place knee implants. Dr. Gaurav Saini specializes in CORI robotic technology for total knee replacement, offering benefits like better alignment, minimal tissue damage, and faster recovery.

How long is the recovery period after knee replacement surgery?

Most patients can resume light daily activities within 3-6 weeks after knee replacement surgery. Full recovery typically takes 3-6 months. Dr. Saini's minimally invasive techniques and personalized rehabilitation plans help accelerate the recovery process.

What sports injuries do you treat?

Dr. Gaurav Saini treats various sports injuries including ACL tears, meniscus injuries, shoulder dislocations, rotator cuff tears, ankle sprains, and tennis elbow. He specializes in minimally invasive arthroscopic procedures for faster healing.
